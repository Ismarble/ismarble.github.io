General Expectations (Still read the rules)

  • Respect Above All: Players are expected to act with integrity and sportsmanship throughout the game. This includes respecting boundaries, homes, workspaces, medical facilities, and fellow competitors. (read the rules)
  • Play Safe: All weapons must meet safety standards outlined in the rules. No part of this game should cause harm, discomfort, or fear to participants or bystanders. (read the rules)
  • Follow the Rules: Every player is responsible for knowing and following all guidelines. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse. (read the rules)
  • Obey the Judge(s): The designated judge(s) have final authority on rule interpretations, disputes, and eligibility. Their calls are final. (read the rules)
  • Use Common Sense: Don’t be reckless, don’t be a nuisance, and don’t make this harder for anyone else. The goal is to have fun and win. (read the rules)
