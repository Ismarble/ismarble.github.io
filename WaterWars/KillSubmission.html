💀 Kill Submission

Kill Tracker — Submit & Score

This has changed from previous years, All confirmed eliminations must be reported using the official BAND GROUP. Accurate and timely submissions help maintain fair scoring and allow judge(s) to verify activity throughout the game. Please upload your photo in the kill submission album and include a comment with the information below.

Submission Requirements

  • Photo Proof:
     See section 5.3. Recording Eliminations in the rules
  • Location:
     List where the kill occurred (e.g. “Legacy Park,” “Front porch of Bobby’s house,” etc.).
  • Time:
     Include the approximate time of elimination. Bonus points apply in the last 2 hours of gameplay (6–8 PM), so accuracy matters (check the time you took your photo proof).
  • Optional Notes:
     Any additional context (e.g., “double points for public park, “on private property,” etc.) can be included.

Important Reminders

  • Submissions without proof may be rejected and must be reviewed before points count.
  • Do not fake kills — suspicious submissions will be investigated.
  • If you were eliminated by someone breaking a rule, report it to the judge(s) immediately. (Text Message or Private BAND Message only.)
