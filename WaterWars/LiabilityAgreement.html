WATER WARS LEGAL PROTECTION AND LIABILITY AGREEMENT

This document applies to all participants, organizers, judges, administrators, content creators, and any individual assisting in the coordination or execution of Water Wars, including any associated team branding, communication platforms, or rule systems. By participating in, observing, assisting, or engaging with any part of the activity, all individuals agree to the following terms.

Water Wars is an entirely unofficial, privately organized recreational activity created for entertainment, strategy gameplay, and social engagement purposes only. It is not affiliated with any school district, municipal government, law enforcement agency, sports organization, or any other formal institution. The activity exists solely as a voluntary, participant-run game. No organizer, judge, or affiliated participant represents any official authority or governmental power.

All participants acknowledge that involvement in Water Wars is completely voluntary. No person is required or compelled to participate, observe, or assist in any capacity. Participation is undertaken at the sole discretion of each individual. By choosing to participate, every player confirms that they understand they are joining a non-sanctioned recreational activity that is not supervised by any coaches, Mentors, certified athletic, medical, or governmental authority.

All participants acknowledge and accept full responsibility for their own safety, conduct, and decisions at all times before, during, and after gameplay. This includes movement through public areas, private property where permitted, and any designated gameplay zones. Participants understand that physical activity carries inherent risks, including but not limited to slips, trips, falls, weather exposure, fatigue, collisions, misunderstandings between players, and minor injuries. Each participant voluntarily assumes all such risks.

Organizers, judges, and administrative contributors are not responsible for any injuries, damages, losses, disputes, or incidents that occur as a result of participation. This includes physical injury, emotional distress, property damage, legal consequences arising from individual actions, or conflicts between players or third parties. All participants agree that they will not hold organizers or judges liable in any civil, criminal, or administrative capacity for outcomes related to gameplay or participation.

All participants further agree that organizers and judges are acting only as voluntary coordinators of gameplay structure. Judges are not referees in any official or legal sense and do not possess any authority outside of the internal rules of Water Wars. All decisions made by judges are strictly for gameplay organization and do not constitute legally binding rulings. Judges may interpret rules, resolve disputes, and assign outcomes strictly for the purpose of maintaining game flow. Such decisions are not subject to external legal challenge within the context of the game structure.

Participants agree that they will not attempt to hold any organizer or judge responsible for the enforcement of rules outside of the gameplay context. All enforcement is symbolic and limited exclusively to in-game consequences as defined by the Water Wars rule system. Any real-world consequences arising from participant actions remain the sole responsibility of the individuals involved.

All participants acknowledge that the “Team Driven” name, branding, identity, and associated materials used in Water Wars are unofficial, student-created, and not affiliated with any commercial entity, trademark holder, or formal organization. Any references to “Team Driven” or similar naming conventions are used strictly as informal identifiers for team organization within the game. No ownership claim over any external trademarks or existing brands is implied. All usage is for non-commercial recreational identification only.

Participants agree that no individual may represent Water Wars, Team Driven, judges, or organizers as an official institution in any external context. This includes communication with schools, employers, property owners, law enforcement, or any third party. Any external communication must clearly indicate the unofficial and recreational nature of the activity when relevant.

All participants agree that they are responsible for ensuring that their involvement in Water Wars does not violate any local laws, property rules, or external regulations. If any participant chooses to engage in gameplay actions that involve public or private spaces, they do so at their own risk and responsibility. Organizers and judges are not responsible for verifying property ownership, permissions, or legal access beyond internal game rules.

Participants acknowledge that property access rules within the game are strictly for gameplay structure and do not override real-world legal requirements. Any permission forms, waivers, or entry agreements used within the game are supplemental to, and not replacements for, actual legal consent required by property owners or guardians.

All participants agree to indemnify and hold harmless all organizers, judges, administrators, and affiliated contributors from any claims, liabilities, damages, costs, or legal actions that may arise from participation in Water Wars. This includes attorney fees, court costs, settlement costs, or any related financial burdens arising from disputes involving gameplay participation or rule interpretation.

Participants acknowledge that Water Wars may involve physical interaction with water-based equipment, including water guns, water balloons, and similar recreational devices. All such equipment is used at the sole risk of the participant. Participants agree that they are responsible for ensuring their equipment is safe, appropriate, and compliant with all internal game rules and external safety standards.

Organizers and judges are not responsible for equipment failure, malfunction, misuse, or modification by any participant. Any injury or damage resulting from equipment use remains the responsibility of the individual using or modifying such equipment.

All participants agree that media content, including photos, videos, audio recordings, and written submissions, may be collected during gameplay for scoring, verification, documentation, or entertainment purposes. By participating, individuals grant permission for such media to be used on Water Wars communication platforms, including group messaging systems, score-tracking systems, and internal documentation channels. No compensation is provided for such usage, and participants waive any claims to ownership or payment related to such media within the context of the game.

Participants also acknowledge that elimination reporting systems, score submissions, and related documentation methods are community-managed tools for gameplay tracking only. These systems are not official records outside of the game environment. Organizers are not responsible for inaccuracies caused by participant reporting, missed submissions, or disputes over proof validity.

All participants agree that cheating, misrepresentation, or intentional rule manipulation is discouraged and may result in internal game penalties. However, such penalties are symbolic and exist solely within the context of the game. Organizers are not responsible for enforcing real-world disciplinary action beyond voluntary participation rules.

Participants acknowledge that Water Wars may include dynamic rule changes, clarifications, or adjustments made by organizers or judges to maintain gameplay balance. All participants agree to accept that rules may evolve over time and that continued participation constitutes acceptance of updated rules as they are communicated through official game channels.

Participants understand that communication platforms used for Water Wars, including messaging applications and group systems, are not official emergency communication systems. They are intended solely for the coordination of gameplay activity. In the event of real-world emergencies, participants are responsible for contacting appropriate emergency services independently.

Participants agree that any disputes arising from gameplay will first be handled internally through judge review or organizer discussion. External escalation of disputes beyond the game structure is outside the scope of Water Wars and remains the responsibility of individuals involved.

This agreement is intended to function as a full acknowledgment of risk, responsibility, and the unofficial status of the activity. If any portion of this document is found unenforceable in a legal context, all remaining portions shall continue to apply to the maximum extent permitted by applicable law.

By participating in Water Wars in any capacity, each individual confirms they have read, understood, and voluntarily agreed to all terms outlined in this document, and acknowledges that the activity is informal, recreational, and fully unofficial in nature.
